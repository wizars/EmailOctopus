The United Arab Emirates Joined the Madrid System for Trademark Registration

Are you a trademark owner looking to expand to the United Arab Emirates and wishing to file a trademark application in that country? We can help you.

From On December 28, 2021, the Madrid Protocol will enter into force in the United Arab Emirates. This means that from this date, filing trademark applications in the UAE will be greatly simplified for foreign applicants from other Madrid protocol signatories.
